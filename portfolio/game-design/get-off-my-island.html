Get Off My Island! Board Game

About this Project

This is a project that I worked on in my theory and design of games class in a group of students.

My Role:

Helped think of ideas for different items and mechanics in the game

Designed the instruction book for the game

Game Premise and Goal:

Get Off My Island is an island survival board game. The premise of the game is that you are trapped on an island after your
ship is wrecked.The goal of the game is to be the player who survives the longest. Players survive by going around the map
and collect materials to craft items to survive. They also try to collect food in order to survive.
